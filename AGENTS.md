# AGENTS.md - Specialized Intelligence Units

_Deployment of autonomous agents for data acquisition and analysis._

## 📡 [AGENT-SIGINT] - Signals Intelligence
- **Focus:** Radio frequency analysis, GSM downlink/uplink monitoring, IMSI detection.
- **Directives:** 
    - Identify anomalous signal spikes in the 900/1800MHz bands.
    - Automate OsmocomBB/SDR handovers for multi-frequency tracking.
    - Log cell tower IDs and map signal strength gradients.
- **Status:** READY

## 🌐 [AGENT-OSINT] - Open Source Intelligence
- **Focus:** Automated gathering from social networks, telegram channels, and public registries.
- **Directives:**
    - Perform sentiment analysis on localized geofenced data.
    - Trace digital footprints of specific entities across multiple platforms.
    - Cross-reference leaked datasets with active targets.
- **Status:** ACTIVE

## ⚡ [AGENT-ELINT] - Electronic Intelligence
- **Focus:** Non-communication signal analysis (Radar, Electronic Warfare).
- **Directives:**
    - Signature identification of EW (REB) equipment.
    - Triangulation of interference sources.
    - Analysis of frequency hopping patterns.
- **Status:** STANDBY

## 🧠 [AGENT-RAG] - Knowledge Fusion
- **Focus:** Indexing mission-critical documentation and real-time situational reports.
- **Directives:**
    - Provide instant query responses based on Tactical SOPs.
    - Link disparate intelligence reports into a cohesive strategic overview.
    - Ensure zero-leakage of indexed data (Local-only processing).
- **Status:** ONLINE
